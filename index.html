What is Hurley's?

Hurley's is a family restaurant in the beautiful town of Maggie Valley, a quaint town nestled in the Great Smokey Mountains, located on "Maggie's" main drag, soco road, along side the mesmerizing Jonathan Creek. Just a couple of minutes from Cataloochee Ski Resort, The Blue Ridge Parkway, Great Smokey Mountain National Park, and all the wonderful views, sights and awesome activities here in our part of the Appalachian Range.

Come as you are.

Just coming off the road, the slopes, the trail, we're not here to judge, we are here to cater to you. While the atmosphere and the experience you will enjoy boasts fancy fine dining, with white linens, candle-lit tables, and crystal everywhere, you are welcome, all of you, we're not snobs, in this economy -who could be, and our prices and our level of hometown hospitality are a testament to that.

From the kitchen.

We serve a wide variety of local and international fares, and use only the best, most fresh and nearest to home as possible, ingredients in all of our dishes, in addition, if we can make it in house we do, our fries are house cut, onion rings -hand breaded, mashed potatoes, potato skins, blooming onions, fried mushrooms, shrimp, salad dressings, dipping sauces, the list goes on, all prepared from scratch by hand in house -it makes a very tasty difference.

Our full service rhum bar.

Dubbed "The Rhum Bar" —because we love rhum, we carry a delicious variety of 13+ different types of rhum. We also carry a wide selection of other spirits including more than a few different types of premium vodka, both flavored and unflavored, as well as many different types of premium gin, tequila, whiskey, scotch, bourbon, brandy, cognac, and a large number of cordials. A little something for everyone. We feature a beautiful range of international wines and local craft beer as well, we are located just outside of beer city USA after all.
